• This artifact was the sole project where I was assigned a chapter of a text and had to design a presentation to teach the rest of my classmates. My chapter was about how to design an effective interface program. I discussed the embodiment principle and decided to use an avatar with my voice as a way of delivering the instruction. I felt that modeling the exact principles I was teaching about was a great method to aid my colleagues in how to create an effective digital learning environment. Looking back, this project was great because it gave me a chance to actually create rather than talk about what I was learning. Designing an effective learning artifact that is suitable for all types of learners will help me keep my learners engaged the first time around and limit the time it takes for stopping to re-teach.
