# About

> [!abstract] Scope and Summary
> This paper presents collection development policy scenarios. The scenarios required me to apply collection development policies and consider factors such as mission statements, intellectual freedom, diversity, equity, and curriculum alignment.
- **AASL Standard:** This project aligns with **ALA/AASL Standard 4.1: Access**.
    - This standard involves my role in developing and implementing policies and procedures to ensure equitable access to resources and services.
    - The scenarios required me to apply collection development policies and consider factors such as diversity, equity, and intellectual freedom.
    - By analyzing these scenarios, the candidate learns to develop and implement policies that promote equitable access to a diverse and inclusive collection.
