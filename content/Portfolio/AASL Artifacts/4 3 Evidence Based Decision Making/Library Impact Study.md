# About

> [!abstract] Scope and Summary
> This report is based on determining the effectiveness of library media specialists in relation to student achievement and motivation. The study examines the impact of school library programs, services, and resources on student learning achievement and motivation, as well as the people’s perceptions of school library media specialists and the influence they have on technology use.
- **AASL Standard**: This project aligns with **ALA/AASL Standard 4.3 Evidence-Based Decision Making**.
    - This standard focuses on using data and information to assess the impact of practice and policy.
    - The assignment focuses on a library impact study that examines the relationship between library media specialists, library programs, and student achievement and motivation.
    - By analyzing the study, I was required to consider evidence of how library practices and policies affect student learning.
