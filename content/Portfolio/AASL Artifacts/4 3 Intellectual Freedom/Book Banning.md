# About

> [!abstract] Scope and Summary
> This document discusses book banning, personal biases, and professional ethics in the context of school libraries. I discuss the potential conflict between a what may be my personal beliefs and my professional responsibility to provide access to diverse perspectives.
- **AASL Standard:** This project aligns with **ALA/AASL Standard 4.4: Intellectual Freedom**.
    - This standard involves my commitment to upholding intellectual freedom by allowing students to have the freedom to have access to a wide variety of selections.
    - The presentation directly addresses the issue of book banning and the importance of providing access to diverse perspectives.
    - It emphasizes my role in defending intellectual freedom and promoting open access to information.
