> [!abstract] Scope and Summary
> This assignment presents a school library community analysis. The analysis includes a description of my library community, data on grade/age level, gender percentage, ethnicity, disabilities, and family life, as well as information on students' technology access, reading preferences, and research preferences.
- **AASL Standard:** This project aligns with **ALA/AASL Standard 1.3: Learning Differences**.
    - This standard involves my role in supporting the diverse needs of learners.
    - The analysis provides detailed information about the school community, including demographics, disabilities, and family life.
    - This information is essential for understanding and addressing the diverse learning needs within my immediate community.
