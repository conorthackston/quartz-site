> [!abstract] Scope and Summary
> This assignment involves an evaluation of the physical facilities of my school library media center at Waterloo Elementary. The evaluation includes assessing the arrangement of furniture, support for collaboration and co-teaching, seating to promote reading, and the integration of technology. I reflected on the positive aspects of the library and suggested improvements, particularly in enhancing access to digital resources and supporting diverse learners.
- **AASL Standard**: This project aligns with **ALA/AASL Standard 1.4 Learning Environment**.
    
    - This standard emphasizes creating learner-centered environments that are engaging and equitable.
    - The assignment required an evaluation of the library's physical space, focusing on elements that contribute to a positive learning environment.
    - I assessed how the arrangement of furniture supports collaboration, co-teaching, and reading.
    - It also involves considering the integration of technology to enhance access to digital resources, promoting equity and engagement throughout the school community.
