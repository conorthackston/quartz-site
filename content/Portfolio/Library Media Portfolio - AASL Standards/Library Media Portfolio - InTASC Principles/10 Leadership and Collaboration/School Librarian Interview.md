# About

> [!abstract] Scope and Summary
> I held this interview with a school library media specialist from another school. The interview covers different topics, including the evolving role of the library media center in the digital age, balancing traditional library services with digital demands, ensuring equitable access to resources, and engaging with families.
- **InTASC Principle: This project aligns with InTASC Principle #10: Leadership and Collaboration.**
    - This principle involves me seeking appropriate leadership roles and opportunities to take responsibility for student learning, to collaborate with learners, families, colleagues, other school professionals, and community members to ensure learner growth, and to advance the profession.
    - The interview provides insights into how an experienced library media specialist is leading and collaborating within their school community.
    - The discussion of adapting to the digital age, ensuring equitable access, and engaging with families highlights the importance of leadership and collaboration in effectively serving the needs of all learners.
