> 4.4c. Collaborate with educators to design accessible and personal digital learning programs that accommodate learner variability.

---

• The next artifact serves as a map for our demonstration of the Personalization and Embodiment Principles. My partner and I collaborated on laying out the foundation for our artifact design. I focused on ensuring the design is accessible to learners of all abilities while also having a personalized approach to engage learners. Overall, this was a great idea because it gave me a chance to plan out our approach and break down the aspects of how to make the project personalized for learners as well as how we can create an embodiment that makes learners feel relaxed and engaged at the same time. This project was highly effective to break down what the artifact will look like before it is fully assembled. I can certainly look back on this framework in the future for how to effectively design a personalized learning module in my own learning environment.
