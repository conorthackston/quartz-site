• This artifact is from Blackboard, which is the Learning Management System that Towson University used throughout our program. This part of Blackboard was the discussion post which every course had us do for three and a half years. This artifact is a representation of all the time I have spent with my classmates sharing information on topics such as new and emerging technologies, how to evaluate those technologies for a classroom setting, while also innovating ways that we teach within our schools. We reviewed each other’s projects, collaborated and shared our designs with one another through these discussion posts. Looking back, I would say it certainly gave us a chance to connect with other motivated professionals in a network built for assisting us in the technology sciences and I can confidently say that I have learned a great deal from this artifact over the last few years. Going forth, it is going to be key for me to continue my education by actively participating in professional learning networks in order to continue honing my educational technology skills.
