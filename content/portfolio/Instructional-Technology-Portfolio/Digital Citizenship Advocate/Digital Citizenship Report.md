• This report focuses on what students are struggling with and where they find strength in the digital citizenship world. The report includes aspects that I would add into the curriculum to assist students’ learning across digital platforms. This can be useful for other educators as a resource of where to look when planning lessons as well as where to find ideas on how to combat students’ weaknesses in a digital world. I will continue to reference this project while working with students in both a classroom and a digital environment. Eventually, these issues may increase as more technology is introduced into the classroom. Therefore, it is important to always step back and reflect on how to manage a digital learning environment within the classroom.
