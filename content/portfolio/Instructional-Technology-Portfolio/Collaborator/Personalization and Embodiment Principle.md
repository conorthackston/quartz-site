• This project is a courseware which is based on the principles that learners value a personalized approach when partaking in learning. When the material is delivered in a friendly and personal manner, learners feel more at-ease and engaged in the material. The embodiment aspect of this project deals with learners being more engaged when there is a friendly avatar to guide them through the projects. This project took the most time because my partner had to record herself speaking and send me the video clips to crop and add to the project. My job was to save the videos that were sent to me and upload them to the course that I created. I organized the look and presentation of the course along with the interactive activities and feedback that was given. Overall, we supported our colleagues in the class because we were the ones assigned the task of these two principles and I believe we did an excellent job at modeling an effective way to use technology to improve student learning. Collaborating with colleagues is something I value since difficult tasks can be accomplished when working together in an effective manner.
