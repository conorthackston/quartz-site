# About

.

> [!abstract] Scope and Summary
> This document presents a mission and vision statement for my school library at Waterloo Elementary. The mission statement focuses on fostering a lifelong love of learning by providing access to diverse resources, innovative programs, and personalized support. The vision statement outlines the library's role in empowering students, supporting teachers, reflecting the community's needs, and promoting literacy and learning.
- **AASL Standard**: This project aligns with **ALA/AASL Standard 5.2 Leadership and Collaboration**.
    - This standard involves leading and collaborating with the learning community to implement solutions that impact learner growth.
    - The assignment required the creation of a mission and vision statement for my school library.
    - These statements are meant to guide Waterloo Elementary library's goals and direction, demonstrating leadership.
