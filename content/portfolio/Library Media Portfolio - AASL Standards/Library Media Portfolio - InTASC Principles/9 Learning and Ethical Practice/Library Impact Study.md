# About

> [!abstract] Scope and Summary
> This report is based on determining the effectiveness of library media specialists in relation to student achievement and motivation. The study examines the impact of school library programs, services, and resources on student learning achievement and motivation, as well as the people’s perceptions of school library media specialists and the influence they have on technology use.
- **InTASC Principle**: This project aligns with **InTASC Standard #9: Professional Learning and Ethical Practice**.
    
    - This principle involves using evidence to evaluate the effects of my own practice.
    - By examining the findings of the study, I am encouraged to reflect on the effectiveness of library services and consider areas for improvement.
    - The presentation also highlights areas of concern, such as the need for more differentiated instruction for students with IEPs, prompting the candidate to think about ethical considerations and the need to adapt practice to meet diverse learner needs.
