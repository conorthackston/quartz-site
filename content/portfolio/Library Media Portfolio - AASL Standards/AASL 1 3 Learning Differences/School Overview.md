### Addressing a Diverse School of Learners

---

> [!abstract] Scope and Summary
> This assignment provides an overview of Waterloo Elementary School. It includes details about the school's demographics, academic achievements, staff, and library media program.
- **AASL Standard**: This project aligns with **ALA/AASL Standard 1.3 Learning Differences**.
    - This standard focuses on cultivating the educational and personal development of all members of a learning community, including those with diverse needs.
    - The assignment gives an overview of my school which has a diverse student population, including a variety of academic levels, socioeconomic backgrounds, and minority representation.
    - It describes the school's efforts to support students with disabilities and ESOL students, indicating a focus on addressing learning differences.
