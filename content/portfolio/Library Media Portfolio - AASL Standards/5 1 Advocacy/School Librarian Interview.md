# About

> [!abstract] Scope and Summary
> I held this interview with a school library media specialist from another school. The interview covers different topics, including the evolving role of the library media center in the digital age, balancing traditional library services with digital demands, ensuring equitable access to resources, and engaging with families.
- **AASL Standard:** This project aligns with **ALA/AASL Standard 5.1: Advocacy**.
    - This standard involves the library professional advocating for the library program to stakeholders.
    - The interview explores how the library media specialist is adapting to the digital age, ensuring equitable access, and engaging with families.
    - These topics relate to advocating for the library's role in the school and community and promoting its value to stakeholders. Throughout the interview we touched on how a librarian is crucial to a school and discussed many things that the average person might not know about being a librarian.
