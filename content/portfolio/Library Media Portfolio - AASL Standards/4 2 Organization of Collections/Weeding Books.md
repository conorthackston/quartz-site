# About

> [!abstract] Scope and Summary
> This document provides a record of weeding books from my Waterloo Elementary library collection. The record includes details such as title, format, publication year, Dewey age, condition, CREW reason (MUSTIE), equity-informed decision, circulation statistics, and the decision to weed or retain. I chose the computer science section since I also teach technology.
- **AASL Standard:** This project aligns with **ALA/AASL Standard 4.2: Organization of collections**.
    - This standard involves my responsibilities in maintaining an organized, up-to-date, and relevant collection.
    - The document details the process of weeding books based on criteria such as condition, currency, and relevance.
    - This process is essential for maintaining an organized and effective library collection that meets the needs of the learning community here at Waterloo Elementary.
