## Accessible Learning

## Differentiate in Clicks, Not Hours: Powered by NotebookLM

The Challenge: The Differentiation Gap Differentiation is often treated as a luxury because it is incredibly time-consuming. Teachers struggle to take a single complex text and make it accessible to English Language Learners (ELL), students with IEPs, and advanced learners simultaneously without spending hours manually rewriting materials.

The Solution: Grounded AI for Universal Design NotebookLM acts as a secure, grounded engine for differentiation. By uploading specific HCPSS curriculum documents, you can instantly generate tiered resources that are 100% aligned with the lesson—eliminating "hallucinations" or irrelevant internet searches.

The Workflow Showcase

- Source Grounding: Upload the core text (PDF/Doc) directly to NotebookLM to ensure the AI only references approved materials.
- Targeted Differentiation: Use specific prompts to generate three versions of the same text:
	- Scaffolded: Simplified vocabulary and shorter sentences for emerging readers.
		- Standard: Grade-level text with embedded comprehension checks.
		- Extension: Socratic questions and debate topics for advanced learners.
- Audio Accessibility: Use the "Audio Overview" feature to convert dense text into an engaging, podcast-style discussion, supporting auditory learners and students with visual processing needs.

Key Benefits

- Equitable Access: Every student accesses the same core concepts, ensuring no one is "locked out" of the grade-level curriculum.
- Verified Accuracy: Unlike general chatbots, NotebookLM answers only using your uploaded documents, maintaining high instructional integrity.
- Multi-Modal Engagement: The audio features transform dry readings into interactive, podcast-style learning experiences.

HCPSS Strategic Plan Alignment

This grounded differentiation workflow serves as a cornerstone for district-wide instructional integrity and operational efficiency.

- Priority 1: Strengthen Learning and Instruction: This initiative specifically fulfills Outcome 1.1 by creating truly equitable learning environments. By automating the "leveling" of complex texts, we effectively "tailor evidence-based interventions" to meet the diverse linguistic and cognitive needs of all students.
- Priority 1.3: High-Quality Instruction: By grounding AI in district-approved materials, we maintain the rigorous standards required by the Board of Education, ensuring that every student engages with the core curriculum—not an "AI hallucination."
- Priority 3: Foster Staff Growth & Engagement: By significantly reducing the "prep-time penalty" associated with manual differentiation, we empower our educators to focus their energy on direct student connection and high-impact instruction.

Strategic Impact: This workflow transforms the "luxury" of differentiation into a standard classroom practice, ensuring that no student is locked out of the grade-level standards due to reading or language barriers.
